# Global Sales Analysis

## Overview
This project features a two-page interactive Excel dashboard built to analyse over 112,000 sales transactions across 6 countries from 2011 to 2016. It uncovers actionable insights on revenue trends, product profitability, regional performance, and customer demographics. The project demonstrates end-to-end data analyst skills, from raw data cleaning and formula correction, to pivot table modelling, interactive dashboard design, and business storytelling.

## Objectives
- Clean and validate a raw dataset of 113,036 records, removing duplicates and correcting a revenue formula error affecting all records
- Analyse sales performance across 3 product categories and 6 countries over 6 years
- Identify high-profit regions, top customer segments, and best-performing product sub-categories
- Track revenue trends over time to reveal seasonal patterns and year-over-year growth
- Communicate findings through a professional two-page dashboard with in-chart insights and business summaries

## Tools & Techniques Used
- Microsoft Excel — Core analytical and visualisation engine
- Pivot Tables — Used for multi-dimensional data summarisation across 10+ pivot tables
- Slicers — Dynamic filtering by Year, Country, Product Category, and Gender with cross-chart connectivity
- Charts — Line charts, clustered bar charts, donut charts, and column charts across 2 dashboard pages
- Data Cleaning — Duplicate removal, revenue formula correction (Unit Price × Order Quantity), and column standardisation
- Calculated Fields — Profit Margin calculated field built directly within pivot tables
  
## Key Insights Discovered
- Overall Financial Performance: The business generated $54M in Total Revenue with a strong 38% Total Profit Margin ($19M Total Profit).
- Peak Revenue Year: 2015 generated $22.3M - a 121% increase from the 2011 baseline of $10.1M, before a decline in 2016 suggesting market saturation
- Product Concentration Risk: Bikes account for 69% of total revenue ($65M) but carry the highest cost base — Accessories deliver a superior 47% profit margin despite lower volume
- Geographic Dependency: The United States leads with $35M (37% of total revenue), with Australia second at $24M — two markets account for 62% of all revenue
- Top State: California generates $7.7M in profit — nearly double the second-placed state (England at $4.4M)
- Key Customer Segment: Adults aged 35–64 drive 50% of total revenue at $25M — over 3x more than any other age group
- Gender Split: Male vs Female revenue is near-equal (51% vs 49%) — confirming effective gender-neutral market appeal
- Seasonal Pattern: Revenue consistently peaks between May and June each year — a clear opportunity for targeted mid-year campaigns
- Highest Margin Sub-Category: Fenders and Helmets deliver the highest profit margins across all sub-categories — a strategic growth opportunity

## How to Interact with the Dashboard
1. Download the raw Excel file from this repository.
2. Open the file in Microsoft Excel.
3. Use the slicers on the right-hand panel to filter by Year, Country, Product Category, or Gender
4. Watch all charts update dynamically based on your selections
